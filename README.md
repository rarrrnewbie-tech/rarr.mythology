<h1>Greek Mythology</h1>
<h2>Primordial Gods (Πρωτογενείς)</h2>
<ul class="god-list">
<li>1. Chaos (Χάος) - the void</li>
<li>2. Gaia (Γαῖα) - the Earth</li>
<li>3. Tartarus (Τάρταρος) - the Underworld</li>
<li>4. Eros (Ἔρως) - love</li>
<li>5. Erebus (Ἔρεβος) - darkness</li>
<li>6. Nyx (Νύξ) - night</li>
<li>7. Aether (Αἴθηρ) - upper air</li>
<li>8. Hemera (Ἡμέρα) - day</li>
<li>9. Pontus (Πόντος) - the sea</li>
<li>10. Ourea (Οὐρέα) - mountains</li>
<li>11. Thalassa (Θάλασσα) - the sea</li>
</ul>

<h2>Titan Gods (Τιτάνες)</h2>
<ul class="god-list">
<li>1. Cronus (Κρόνος) - time</li>
<li>2. Rhea (Ρέα) - fertility</li>
<li>3. Oceanus (Ωκεανός) - the ocean</li>
<li>4. Hyperion (Υπερίων) - light</li>
<li>5. Iapetus (Ιαπετός) - mortality</li>
<li>6. Coeus (Κοῖος) - intellect</li>
<li>7. Phoebe (Φοίβη) - the moon</li>
<li>8. Tethys (Τηθύς) - the sea</li>
<li>9. Theia (Θεία) - sight</li>
<li>10. Themis (Θέμις) - justice</li>
<li>11. Mnemosyne (Μνημοσύνη) - memory</li>
<li>12. Crius (Κρῖος) - constellations</li>
</ul>

<h2>Olympian Gods (Ὄλυμπος)</h2>
<ul class="god-list">
<li>1. Zeus (Ζεύς) - king of the gods</li>
<li>2. Poseidon (Ποσειδῶν) - god of the sea</li>
<li>3. Hades (Ἅιδης) - god of the underworld</li>
<li>4. Hera (Ἑρα) - queen of the gods</li>
<li>5. Demeter (Δημήτηρ) - goddess of agriculture</li>
<li>6. Athena (Ἀθηνά) - goddess of wisdom</li>
<li>7. Apollo (Ἀπόλλων) - god of the sun, music, and prophecy</li>
<li>8. Artemis (Ἀρτέμις) - goddess of the hunt and wilderness</li>
<li>9. Ares (Ἄρης) - god of war</li>
<li>10. Aphrodite (Ἀφροδίτη) - goddess of love</li>
<li>11. Hephaestus (Ἡφαῖστος) - god of fire and craftsmanship</li>
<li>12. Hermes (Ἑρμῆς) - messenger of the gods</li>
<li>13. Dionysus (Διόνυσος) - god of wine and festivals</li>
</ul>
